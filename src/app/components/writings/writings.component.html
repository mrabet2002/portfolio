Exploring the Dynamics of AI Innovation: Insights from the GEIW Conference

During the GEIW days, I had the opportunity to participate in the conference titled “Artificial Intelligence: Lever of Innovation and Entrepreneurial Dynamics.” This event explored the applications of artificial intelligence (IA) across various domains, highlighting its impact on entrepreneurial dynamics in the AI era. Key discussions included the integration of AI in management and its transformative potential. The conference featured expert presentations on topics ranging from generative AI to the intersection of AI and IoT. This essay summarizes the key insights and takeaways from this enlightening event.

The event began with Mr. Mohammed Youssfi, who presented on generative AI and prompt engineering. He highlighted the principles of responsible AI and discussed how to leverage generative AI through few-shot learning, fine-tuning, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Mr. Youssfi's presentation concluded with a demonstrative application of RAG, showcasing its practical benefits.

Following this, a representative from EMSI discussed the integration of AI with IoT and the transition from Industry 4.0 to Industry 5.0. He presented several projects his team had developed, including an offline AI application designed to enhance data security within a company, particularly using AI bots.

The next speaker, an HR manager, shared insights from his experience in HR management. He focused on how AI is transforming the recruitment process, notably through AI bots that can conduct interviews. However, he emphasized the significant difference between AI and humans—emotions—which AI does not consider in interviews.

In conclusion, the conference was an enriching experience that offered a comprehensive overview of the potential and challenges of AI. It highlighted the transformative power of AI across various sectors and underscored the importance of responsible AI practices. The insights gained from the presentations emphasized the need for continued innovation and ethical considerations in the development and application of AI technologies. Participating in this conference not only broadened my understanding of AI but also inspired me to explore further how AI can drive innovation and improve efficiency in various fields.

Professional Communication: Insights from S4 English Session

Professional communication is a critical skill set that encompasses various forms, including written, verbal, and digital communication. In my English class, I learned about the importance of professional communication and its different aspects, such as digital networking, written documents like resumes and cover letters, and verbal communication in interviews.

Digital communication and professional networking are essential in the job search process. We discussed the hidden job market, where job seekers utilize their connections to find job opportunities, often leading to more direct and successful interviews. Additionally, we learned about the advertised job market, where companies post job openings on platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed. Cold calling was also highlighted as a proactive approach to inquire about potential job openings directly with companies.

In the context of written communication, we focused on creating professional documents like resumes and cover letters. We analyzed a sample resume, discussing its components and how to tailor it to specific job applications. Similarly, we learned about the importance of customizing cover letters to highlight relevant skills and experiences for each job application.

Verbal communication skills are crucial during job interviews. We learned about common interview questions and strategies for answering them effectively. Emphasis was placed on researching the company before the interview to tailor responses and demonstrate genuine interest in the position.

Overall, the S4 sessions of the English class provided valuable insights into the importance of professional communication in various forms. From digital networking to crafting tailored resumes and cover letters, and mastering interview skills, the course equipped me with practical knowledge and skills essential for navigating the professional world.
